Inspire Excellence

ETQ Quality Vision is ETQ’s new spring customer conference, making its first annual appearance in sunny Miami, Florida. ETQ Quality Vision 2020 is your opportunity to learn about the latest ETQ solutions and innovations, connect with fellow ETQ customers from around the world and share experiences and best practices. This spring’s conference will also focus on how to drive quality throughout your organization — from suppliers to production, your customers and, ultimately, to your customers’ customer. Together, we’ll advance the conversation around the business value of quality and share actionable insights on how to inspire a culture of quality that will grow brand loyalty and accelerate revenue at your company.

At the ETQ Quality Vision 2020 in Miami you will:

  • Expand your knowledge in hands-on sessions and training on ETQ solutions
  • Learn best practices from fellow quality champions
  • Build connections through networking and interactive discussions
  • Understand how quality can be a business differentiator
  • Schedule interactive one-on-one consultations with ETQ experts
  • Learn how to maximize the return on your investment in ETQ Reliance

  • Quality Leadership Forum: A half day of networking, industry expert and quality leader sessions and discussion. Learn from other quality leaders, discuss key industry trends, and gain insights on executing a quality strategy. Learn more

  • Knowledge Sharing: Learn from customers about their quality journey in areas such as supply chain, EHS&Q, and integrations. Further, hear from ETQers on how to get the most out of your Reliance system, product updates, and the roadmap.

ETQ Passport Training

As part of Quality Vision 2020, the ETQ Reliance Passport Training will be held on Thursday and Friday, where you can participate in advanced training on ETQ Reliance. This training includes administrator training, configurations and advanced features for those looking to further their Reliance knowledge. Watch a brief training sessions overview video to learn more about your training options. You can also register for only the Passport Training.

Class Levels: Administration (hands-on), Designer, ETQScript, Advanced/Expert & Mobile Designer (New)

ETQ Reliance Administration (Named-Caller) Hands-On

The Administration (Named-Caller) course provides a hands-on overview of day-to-day administration activities of ETQ Reliance. Exercises will cover how to best utilize the Administration Center to manage users, groups, and security. Content will also cover how best to work with ETQ Technical Support via the ETQ Help Desk and common administrative tasks such as promotions, reviewing logs, etc.

Prerequisites

Successful completion of the following courses in ETQ Academy:

  • ETQ Reliance Administrator Certification Training 2019 (~4hrs)

Who should take this course?

  • Reliance Administrators / Named-Callers who would like to understand how to efficiently work with ETQ Technical Support
  • Reliance Administrators / Named-Callers responsible for the day-to-day administration and support of their Reliance instance

ETQ Reliance Designer – Designing and Building Applications

The Introduction to ETQ Reliance Designer course provides a comprehensive and hands-on overview of configuration utilizing the Reliance Designer. Exercises will cover how to build forms, workflows, and views in ETQ Reliance as well as how best to utilize the Administration Center to develop and modify basic documents and processes.

Prerequisites

Successful completion of the following courses in ETQ Academy:

  • ETQ Reliance Administrator Certification Training 2019 (~4hrs)
  • ETQ Reliance Basic Designer Certification Training 2019 (~6hrs)

Who should take this course?

  • Reliance Administrators who would like to better understand how the back-end of their Reliance system functions
  • Beginning Reliance Designers who would like an opportunity to have a guided experience in building and modifying forms, workflows, and views
  • Named Callers who need a better understanding of system design elements to be able to better troubleshoot issues or work with the ETQ Help Desk

ETQScript – Configuring Advanced Business Logic in ETQ Reliance

The ETQScript course will cover hands-on ETQ Scripting exercises to help customers configure more complex business requirements. The exercises will specifically focus on using ETQScript to configure a module to apply commonly requested business logic.

Prerequisites

Experience with programming languages (e.g. Python, Java, etc) strongly recommended

Successful completion of the following courses in ETQ Academy:

  • ETQ Reliance Basic Designer Certification Training 2019 (~4hrs)
  • ETQ Reliance Administrator Certification Training 2019 (~6hrs)
  • ETQ Reliance Intermediate Designer Certification Training 2019 (~4hrs)

Who should take this course?

  • Proficient Reliance Designers who would like an opportunity to have hands-on experience in writing and modifying ETQScript to meet business needs
  • Named Callers/Administrators who need a better understanding of how to troubleshoot and update ETQScript logic

Advanced/Expert ETQ Reliance Implementation Use Cases and Case Studies

An interactive session covering multiple business cases requiring advanced ETQ configuration and scripting skills. The session covers best practices around scripting and explains a step by step approach in achieving some highly complex business requirements using ETQ Reliance. Solutions sets cover the entire gamut of ETQ’s advanced solution offerings, such as real-time integrations for just-in-time delivery, advanced scripting to automate compliance and quality assurance and automated problem detection to enable a pro-active approach to problem solving. The purpose of the session is to learn how to maximize Reliance to solve the trickiest and most complex business requirements.

Prerequisites

Successful completion of the following courses in ETQ Academy:

  • ETQ Reliance Basic Designer Certification Training 2019 (~4hrs)
  • ETQ Reliance Administrator Certification Training 2019 (~6hrs)
  • ETQ Reliance Intermediate Designer Certification Training 2019 (~4hrs)

Minimum 2 years experience in managing and configuring ETQ Reliance applications

Who should take this course?

  • Advanced Reliance Administrators and Designers who have advanced knowledge of ETQ Reliance and related technical knowledge in databases, programming, and enterprise integration

Introduction to ETQ Mobile Designer: NEW

The Mobile Designer course provides a comprehensive and hands-on overview of configuration utilizing the Reliance Mobile Designer. Exercises will cover how to build mobile forms, workflows, and views for already configured processes in ETQ Reliance.

Prerequisites

Successful completion of the following courses in ETQ Academy:

  • ETQ Reliance Administrator Certification Training 2019 (~4hrs)
  • ETQ Reliance Basic Designer Certification Training 2019 (~6hrs)

Who should take this course?

  • Reliance Designers interested in deploying mobile functionality for their Reliance implementation
