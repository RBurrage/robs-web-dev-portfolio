Responsive Form with JavaScript Validation

I created this form for the American Tower Brazil website. I wrote two versions (English and Portuguese) in HTML5, validated with JavaScript, and used PHP to send the form data to an internal mailbox. The JavaScript calls error messages in the correct language depending on which site the form is on.

English: http://www.americantower.com.br/en/contact-us/index.php
Portuguese: http://www.americantower.com.br/en/contact-us/index.php

Dynamically Change Contact Person Depending on Time Zone

I wrote this script for a training library I created at American Tower. The site offers round-the-clock support for end users. The script dynamically changes the contact name to "Robin Burrage during U.S. business hours and to "Gaurav Kumar" during business hours in India.
